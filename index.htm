Welcome!

This is the first online database focused on cataloging programmed ribosomal frameshift signals (PRF) in eukaryotic genomes. Why would we want to do that? Follow the "Information" link. If you want to find out if your favorite gene contains a frameshift signal (FSS), then follow the link "Database Tools". Under the "Resources" link, you will find an (old, but usefull) PowerPoint presentation which details the motivation of this website. There is also a link there pointing to Jonathan Jacobs' Resource Page (that's me).
